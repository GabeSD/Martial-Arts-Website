Here are bellcurve charts that show the results.In these charts you can see that Martial Artists were beat at the visual reaction time test, but not the auditory reaction time test.

The Goal

The goal of this expiriment is to test the reaction time and awareness of Martial Artists and compare that to regular day people. I categorized these different people as Gamers, Athletes, and Day-to-day people. It is categorized in this way to specify the type of groups of regular day people because these types affect reaction time and awareness in different ways. It is also to see if these types of people can prove to have more "survivability" than Martial Artits.

The Studio That Helped

Karate 4 Kids USA has helped in gathering the data for this expiriment.
