Results Reaction Time

Here are averages that show the results of the reaction time testing. In these numbers you can see that Martial Artists were beat at the visual reaction time test, but not at the auditory reaction time test.

These tests are averaged in milliseconds because of how the virtual test calculated the subjects. The Martial Artists seem to be slower at these visual reaction test than the Gamers and Athletes.


Results Awareness

Here are averages that show the results of awareness in the three groups. In these numbers you can see that Martial Artists were more aware than the other groups.

The first test shows the average of the visual awareness test. It shows how many of the people counted either above or below 13 passes. In the second test you can see that the Martial Artists have a higher percentage of identifying people around them using only their ears.
